Privacy Policy

AFT Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

The app does not require or use any form of login, location data, analytics, or user identifiers. No information is collected or shared with third parties.

This app contains no advertisements, trackers, or background data collection. It functions fully offline and respects your privacy by design.
